How available are the web platform's features?

This lil' webapp indicates the percentage of users who have a browser that natively supports various web platform features.

It's powered by data from caniuse.com, updated March 30, 2021, and StatCounter (which, admittedly, has its problems).

Obviously, the numbers below only consider native support and not polyfills or other fallback scenarios.
